Culture Landmarks Food
Pizza
Pizza Originating from Naples, pizza has become a global favorite. The classic Margherita pizza, with its simple combination of tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, basil, and olive oil, is an enduring classic. However, the world of pizza toppings in Italy is diverse and delicious.
Pasta
Pasta Pasta is at the heart of Italian cuisine. From spaghetti to lasagna, ravioli to penne, Italy offers a vast variety of pasta dishes, often paired with rich, flavorful sauces like marinara, Alfredo, or pesto. Classics like Spaghetti Carbonara and Fettuccine Alfredo are favorites.
Gelato
Gelato Italian ice cream, known as gelato, is a sweet and creamy treat that's adored worldwide. It comes in a plethora of flavors, from the traditional chocolate and vanilla to unique choices like pistachio, tiramisu, and fruity sorbets. Gelato is a must-try dessert in Italy.
